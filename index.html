Abby is a beautiful, six-and-a-half-year-old black and white cat. We adopted her five years ago.

Welcome to Abby's Page!

Her Life

Abby loves hunting, eating, and sleeping. She knows how to make herself understood when she wants something, whether it's food, playtime, or going outside.

Her Habits

Abby enjoys relaxing in the sun and prefers playing with us rather than with toys. She loves warm and cozy spots, like clothes left on the floor. She's not always a fan of cuddles, but she loves kisses. She only accepts cuddles when we're wearing bathrobes or blankets. And she has a strong preference: she only drinks water from the tap!
